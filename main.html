Our Story


We are entrepreneurs focused on solving your daily charging anxiety.

Our dream is to provide you a high quality and convenient charging service to relieve your daily "charging anxiety" If you are constantly monitoring what percentage of power you have left in your devices because it is critical to your lifestyle to have a fully charged battery, then we have a solution to your dilemma. The new X-1 Patent pending product will eliminate this "charging anxiety" and enable you to go through your day without concern that your battery will let you down during a critical time.

Your contribution can help us commercialize our nearly developed prototype, "X-1”. We need your help to take the X-1 project to the next step. By contributing to the X-1 seed fund raising you will help us create a solution to your ‘charging anxiety’.



"X-1" (Patent Pending)


X-1 is a wireless charging power bank pocket that allows you to recharge your iPhone 5s/6/6s after closing the pocket. X-1 is light, convenient and made of leather.

Development Goals

  • LED Indicator: Blinking speed indicates level of charge
  • Size & Weight: Smaller and lighter
  • Material: Made of higher quality leather with more aesthetically pleasing design
  • Charging Efficiency: 1% ~ 2% per minute
  • Battery Capacity: 3000 ~ 5000 mAh (iPhone 6 has battery capacity equal to 1810 mAh)
  • Strap Holder: A pocket/card holder
  • Product Specialization: Specialize for different group of users
